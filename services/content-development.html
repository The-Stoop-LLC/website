FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of content do you produce?

Short-form video (Reels, TikTok), long-form video, professional photography, drone footage, UGC, copywriting, and branded content series.

Do you provide the equipment?

Yes — our production team comes fully equipped with professional cameras, lighting, audio, stabilizers, and drones.

Can I use the content for paid ads?

Yes — all content is delivered in multiple formats (9x16, 16x9, 1x1) optimized for social, web, and paid media use.

What does a typical shoot look like?

We handle pre-production (storyboarding, shot lists), production (on-location shoots typically 3-8 hours), and post-production (editing, color grading, audio, branding).
