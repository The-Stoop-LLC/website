FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms do you run ads on?

We manage campaigns across Meta (Facebook & Instagram), Google (Search, Display, YouTube), TikTok, and LinkedIn. We recommend platforms based on your audience and goals.

How much should I budget for ad spend?

We've managed monthly ad budgets from $1,500 to $10,000+. The right budget depends on your goals, industry, and target audience. We'll recommend a budget during our discovery call.

How quickly will I see results?

Most campaigns begin generating data within the first week. We optimize continuously and provide monthly performance reports with actionable insights.

Do you create the ad creative too?

Yes — we handle creative development including ad copy, graphics, video assets, and A/B testing variations.
