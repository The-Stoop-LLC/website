FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a brand package?

Logo design, color palette, typography, iconography, brand guidelines document, social media templates, and application examples.

How many logo concepts do you present?

We typically present 4-6 initial concepts, then refine your top choice through 2 rounds of revisions.

Can you design for both digital and print?

Yes — we deliver assets optimized for social media, web, email, business cards, flyers, brochures, and other print materials.

Do you create ongoing campaign creative?

Yes — beyond brand identity, we design campaign-specific creative including ad graphics, social templates, and promotional materials.
