FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you find and vet influencers?

We source creators based on audience demographics, engagement rates, content quality, and brand alignment. Every creator is vetted before activation — we review their content history, audience authenticity, and past brand partnerships.

What platforms do you run influencer campaigns on?

We primarily execute on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Facebook. Platform selection depends on your target audience and campaign goals.

How much does an influencer campaign cost?

Campaign budgets vary based on the number of creators, deliverables, and usage rights. We've managed campaigns from $2,300 to $10,000+ in influencer spend alone, plus management fees.

How do you measure influencer campaign success?

We track views, impressions, engagements, link clicks, CPM, CPC, and conversion events. Every campaign ends with a detailed post-mortem report with actionable insights.
