SYLVAN GARDENS

Organic Social Media Management & Content Development

Sylvan Gardens is a premier landscaping company based in Ingomar, PA, specializing in landscape design, installation, and maintenance for residential and commercial clients across the Pittsburgh area. The Stoop managed their organic social media presence across Facebook and Instagram — producing content, managing the community, and delivering monthly analytics — under a long-term retainer.

The Challenge

Sylvan Gardens had built a strong reputation through word-of-mouth and quality craftsmanship — but their social media presence didn't reflect the caliber of their work. They needed a partner who could manage daily social content, showcase their landscape projects, spotlight team members, and build an engaged community on Facebook and Instagram.

The Results

Over 9+ months of management, The Stoop delivered a combined 38,000+ total impact across Facebook and Instagram, grew the audience by 275 net followers, and produced consistent, professional content that showcased Sylvan Gardens' craftsmanship. The team spotlight series and project showcases became the highest-performing content formats, with posts like 'Meet Jesus,' 'An Incredible Stone Patio Transformation,' and 'Meet the Green Team' driving the strongest engagement. Monthly analytics reporting gave Sylvan Gardens clear visibility into what was working and where to double down.
