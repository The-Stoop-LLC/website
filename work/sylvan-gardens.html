SYLVAN GARDENS

Full-Service Social Media & Digital Advertising

Sylvan Gardens is a premier landscaping company based in Ingomar, PA, specializing in landscape design, installation, and maintenance for residential and commercial clients across the Pittsburgh area. The Stoop serves as their ongoing digital partner — managing social media, running paid campaigns, producing content, and building landing pages under a long-term retainer.

The Challenge

Sylvan Gardens had built a strong reputation through word-of-mouth and quality craftsmanship — but their digital presence didn't reflect the caliber of their work. Their social media accounts were underperforming, with declining reach on Facebook and minimal engagement across platforms.

They needed a partner who could manage the full scope of their digital marketing — from daily social posts to paid advertising campaigns — while producing the visual content needed to showcase their landscape design and installation work to a broader audience.

Our Approach

The Stoop operates as Sylvan Gardens' full-stack digital marketing department — handling everything from content calendars and daily posts to multi-platform paid campaigns and on-site content production.

  • Social media management across Facebook and Instagram with monthly content calendars
  • Google Ads campaigns — display, search, YouTube, and remarketing
  • Meta Ads campaigns — awareness, consideration, and lead generation
  • On-site photography and videography of landscape projects and team operations
  • Social media analysis and strategy with full-year analytical reporting
  • Web development — landing pages and testimonial widgets
  • HR and hiring campaigns to support team growth
  • Hashtag strategy and engagement playbooks

The Results

Under The Stoop's management, Sylvan Gardens' Instagram reach more than doubled year-over-year — growing from 374 to 837 accounts reached — while profile visits surged from 168 to 1,300. The diversified content strategy covering installations, maintenance, employee spotlights, and seasonal campaigns gave Sylvan a consistent, professional presence across platforms.

The long-term retainer model has allowed The Stoop to build deep institutional knowledge of Sylvan's business, refine campaigns over time, and scale from social media management into paid advertising and web development — proving that sustained partnerships deliver compounding results.
