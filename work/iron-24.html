IRON 24

Content Production & Influencer Activation

Iron 24 is a fitness facility with locations in Pearland, TX and Pittsburgh's South Hills. The Stoop delivered content production across both locations — videography, photography, droneography, and educational video series — plus activated 8 local influencers through membership gifting for UGC and organic brand exposure.

The Challenge

Iron 24 needed professional content across two locations — their corporate headquarters in Pearland, TX and a new franchise location in Pittsburgh's South Hills neighborhood owned by Sam Pitcairn. They required cinematic video, professional photography, aerial drone footage, and educational content — all produced to a standard that could fuel their social media, website, and marketing channels.

Engagement 1 — South Hills, PA

Local Franchise

A full content production engagement for Iron 24's South Hills franchise, owned by Sam Pitcairn. Deliverables included a gym tour video, 5 assessment reels, 10 gym reels, and a complete photo set — $2,875 total production value.

Our Approach

  • Produced gym tour video and educational content series for the local franchise
  • Delivered 20 videos, 60 photos, and aerial drone footage for corporate
  • Managed full pre-production, production, and post-production pipeline
  • Color grading, audio mixing, text overlay, and branding across all deliverables
  • Activated 8 local influencers via membership gifting for organic UGC
  • Delivered all content in multiple formats (9x16, 16x9, 1x1)
  • Executed under a formal Master Services Agreement
  • Team: Mike Pernice (Account Manager) + Jason Robinson (Multimedia Producer)

The Results

The Stoop delivered a total production value of $7,875 across two locations — giving Iron 24 a comprehensive content library spanning 20+ videos, 60+ photos, drone footage, and 15 reels. The influencer activation through the South Hills shoot generated organic brand exposure across 8 influencer accounts, establishing Iron 24's presence in the Pittsburgh fitness community before the franchise even fully launched.
