The Challenge

Iron 24 Holdings was preparing to launch a new fitness facility and needed professional content that matched the scale of their ambition. They required a production partner who could handle everything from cinematic video to aerial drone footage — and deliver it on a tight pre-launch timeline.

Beyond content production, Iron 24 needed a social media strategy that would build anticipation before doors opened and sustain momentum after launch.

Our Approach

The Stoop operated as Iron 24's end-to-end content production arm — handling pre-production planning, on-site shoots, post-production editing, and social media strategy. Everything was formalized under a Master Services Agreement for clear deliverables and accountability.

  • Professional videography covering facility tours, equipment showcases, and brand storytelling
  • High-resolution photography for marketing materials, social media, and web
  • Aerial droneography capturing the full scope of the facility and location
  • Social media management proposals for pre-launch, launch, and post-launch phases
  • Formal Master Services Agreement establishing scope, timelines, and retainer terms
  • Full post-production including editing, color grading, and format optimization

The Results

The Stoop delivered a comprehensive content library — 20 professional videos and 60 photos — that gave Iron 24 a premium visual foundation for every marketing channel. The drone footage added a cinematic dimension that set their brand apart from competitors.

The formal MSA established a long-term partnership framework, positioning The Stoop as Iron 24's ongoing content and social media partner through their growth phase.
