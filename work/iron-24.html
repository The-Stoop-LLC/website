The Challenge

Iron 24 needed professional content across two locations — their corporate headquarters in Pearland, TX and a new franchise location in Pittsburgh's South Hills neighborhood owned by Sam Pitcairn. They required cinematic video, professional photography, aerial drone footage, and educational content — all produced to a standard that could fuel their social media, website, and marketing channels.
