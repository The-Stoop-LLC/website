SXP

Brand Video Production & Multi-Brand Partnership

Author: Mike Pernice, Founder · Last updated: April 2026

SXP is a sports performance clinic providing personalized 1-on-1 and group training for athletes at the high school, college, and professional levels. Located within X Shadyside in Pittsburgh, SXP operated as its own business venture. The Stoop produced two brand videos for SXP's website and social media presence, and brokered a multi-brand partnership between SXP, X Shadyside, and Lucid Juice.

The Challenge

SXP needed professional video content that communicated their training philosophy and facility to potential athletes and their families. As a sports performance clinic operating within X Shadyside, they also needed to establish their own distinct brand identity. The Stoop produced two brand videos and connected SXP into a multi-brand partnership that created cross-promotional value for everyone involved.

Our Approach

  • Produced two brand videos showcasing SXP's training facility and philosophy
  • Delivered videos optimized for website hero placement and social media distribution
  • Brokered a multi-brand partnership between SXP, X Shadyside, and Lucid Juice
  • Partnership included member discounts at Lucid Juice for SXP and X Shadyside members
  • Created cross-promotional value across three Pittsburgh businesses

The Results

The two brand videos gave SXP a professional visual identity for their website and social channels, helping them stand out as a distinct brand within X Shadyside's facility. The multi-brand partnership with Lucid Juice created a tangible member benefit — discounted juice for SXP and X Shadyside members — while opening cross-promotional opportunities across all three brands.
