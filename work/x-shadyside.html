How We Did It

Our Process

01

Sourcing

Sourced 50+ Pittsburgh-area fitness, lifestyle, and student-creator influencers across two seasons. Vetted for audience fit, engagement quality, and brand alignment.

02

Activation

Activated 15 influencers across two seasons under collaborative reel + story + likeness-rights deals. Onboarded each with brief, hashtags, and deliverable timelines.

03

Production

3-hour content shoots on location at the facility for each campaign. Produced reels, photos, story posts, and a paid ad reel with full brand framing.

04

Distribution & Reporting

Multi-platform posting (Instagram + Facebook), paid amplification, and full campaign reporting on views, engagements, link clicks, and CPM.
