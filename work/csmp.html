Social Media Management & Content Production

Case Specific Meal Prep is a Pittsburgh-based meal prep and catering company founded by Vince, with Chef Dom crafting personalized, healthy dishes for clients across the city. The Stoop managed their full social media presence, produced food photography and spotlight video content, designed catering menus, and coordinated influencer kitchen collaborations.

The Challenge

Case Specific Meal Prep had built a loyal customer base through word-of-mouth and Chef Dom's incredible dishes, but their social media presence didn't reflect the quality of their product. They needed a partner who could tell their story visually — from the kitchen to the customer — and build the kind of consistent, engaging content that turns followers into orders.

Beyond social media, CSMP was expanding into catering and needed professional menu designs and a content strategy that could support both their meal prep subscriptions and their growing catering business.

Our Approach

The Stoop built a comprehensive content ecosystem for CSMP — managing daily social media posts, producing professional food photography, and creating a themed weekly content calendar that kept the brand consistent and engaging.

  • Weekly content calendar with themed series — Chef's Choice, Ingredient Spotlight, Vendor Spotlight, Client Testimonials, Healthy Tips
  • Professional food photography showcasing Chef Dom's dishes and meal prep process
  • Spotlight video production featuring the team and kitchen operations
  • Influencer kitchen collaborations — creators cooking alongside Chef Dom
  • Catering menu design — breakfast, lunch/dinner, and in-flight menus
  • Interactive social media engagement strategy (polls, Q&As, reminders)
  • Brand identity and logo design

The Results

The Stoop transformed CSMP's social media from sporadic posts into a consistent, professionally produced content machine. The themed content series gave the brand a recognizable voice — from Chef Dom's Choice spotlights to Influencer in the Kitchen collaborations — driving engagement and building a community around healthy, personalized nutrition.

The catering menu designs opened a new revenue stream, giving CSMP the professional collateral needed to pitch corporate catering and private events. The combination of consistent content, professional food photography, and strategic engagement turned social followers into paying customers.
