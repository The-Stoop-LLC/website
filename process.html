How We Work

FROM CONVERSATION TO RESULTS

A clear, repeatable process that takes you from "what do we need?" to "look what we built."

Author: Mike Pernice, Founder

Every engagement follows the same 4-step framework. Whether it's a single influencer campaign or a full-year marketing program, the structure stays consistent. It's how we deliver predictable results without surprises.

01

DISCOVERY

We start by listening. Every project begins with a free consultation where we learn your business, your goals, your audience, and your budget. No pitch deck. No sales pressure. Just a real conversation about what you need.

  • What happens: 30-60 minute call or meeting
  • We learn: Your business model, target audience, competitors, past marketing efforts, and budget
  • You learn: Whether we're the right fit, what services apply, and what results are realistic
  • Deliverable: Custom proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing within 48 hours
02

STRATEGY

Once we're aligned, we build the blueprint. This is the most important step — strategy determines whether your budget works hard or gets wasted. We don't skip this for any engagement, no matter how small.

  • What happens: Deep-dive research into your market, audience, and platform data
  • For influencer campaigns: Influencer sourcing, vetting, rate negotiation, and content brief development
  • For paid media: Audience architecture, platform selection, creative concepts, and budget allocation
  • For organic social: Content pillar development, posting calendar, hashtag strategy, and competitor analysis
  • Deliverable: Strategy document + content calendar, signed off before any execution begins
03

EXECUTE

We do the work. Production, posting, campaign management, optimization — all handled by our team. You stay in the loop with regular check-ins but don't need to manage the day-to-day.

  • Content production: Professional shoots with cinema-grade gear (Sony Fx3, drones, lighting)
  • Influencer management: Contract execution, content approval workflows, posting coordination
  • Paid media: Campaign launch, A/B testing, real-time optimization, budget pacing
  • Organic social: Daily posting, community management, engagement, and story content
  • Client touchpoint: Weekly or bi-weekly syncs depending on engagement scope
04

REPORT

Every dollar gets accounted for. We deliver detailed analytics showing exactly what your investment produced — benchmarked against industry standards so you know if your results are good, great, or exceptional.

  • Metrics tracked: Views, impressions, engagements, link clicks, CPM, CPC, CTR, conversions, ROI
  • Benchmarking: Your results compared against industry averages so performance has context
  • Actionable insights: What worked, what didn't, and what to do next
  • Deliverable: End-of-campaign report (projects) or monthly analytics report (retainers)
  • What's next: Strategy recommendations for the next phase based on real data

What Makes Us Different

  • No work starts without a signed strategy. We never wing it. Every campaign has a plan before a single dollar is spent or a single post goes live
  • We use real data, not vibes. Every recommendation is backed by platform analytics, campaign benchmarks, and audience data
  • You own everything we create. Content, strategies, reports — it's all yours once paid for. No hostage situations
  • We're small on purpose. You work directly with the founder and senior team, not junior account managers
  • Transparent pricing, transparent reporting. No hidden fees, no inflated metrics. See our pricing page for ranges
